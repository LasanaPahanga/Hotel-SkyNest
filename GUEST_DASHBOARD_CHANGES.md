## Design Principles

1. **Contrast:** Strong black and white contrast for visual impact
2. **Typography:** Bold, uppercase headings with generous letter-spacing
3. **Spacing:** Generous padding and margins for breathing room
4. **Borders:** Thick (3px) black borders for definition
5. **Hover Effects:** Transform and shadow effects for interactivity
6. **Consistency:** Unified design language across all elements

## Responsive Design
- Mobile-friendly grid layouts
- Stacks columns on smaller screens
- Maintains readability and usability on all devices
